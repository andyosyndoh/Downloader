Exploring Gemini AI at Build With AI Kisumu

Written By:
John Eliud Odhiambo
Published On:
May 23, 2024
Share

On May 4th 2024, we gathered at the University of Nairobi Kisumu Campus for the GDG-hosted Build with AI, an event focussed on integrating Gemini AI into applications via an Application Programming Interface (API) key. The event showcased how AI can generate valuable information from text prompts. I will provide an overview of the event. I will highlight two intriguing one-on-one sessions. First, I will recap the creation of the Gemiverse application using Flutter/Dart and, secondly, I will speak about the development of Outbard application using Python.

Session 1: Building Gemiverse with Flutter/Dart - Olvine George

The first session of the event focused on Gemiverse; an application developed using Flutter. Gemiverse serves as a comprehensive app that not only provides users with detailed information about their prompts but also generates captivating art from text prompts. This fusion of AI and creative expression opens up a world of opportunities for artists and enthusiasts.

During the session, we were guided through the process of integrating the Gemini AI into the Flutter framework. This integration allowed Gemiverse to harness the power of AI to analyze prompts and retrieve relevant information.

The session emphasized the practical implementation of the Gemini AI, showcasing how developers can use AI to enhance user experiences and explore the boundaries of creativity. We were encouraged to experiment with different prompts and observe the AI's ability to generate accurate information.

Session 2: Building Outbard with Python - Collins Odhiambo

The second session delved into the development of Outbard, an application built with Python. This tool provided users with in-depth information about their prompts, acting as a knowledge companion powered by AI.

Using Gemini AI, Outbard demonstrated how AI can analyze and interpret prompts to generate valuable insights. Attendees learned how to leverage the Gemini AI's functionalities, enabling Outbard to retrieve comprehensive information and present it in a user-friendly manner by building the tools along with the speaker.

The session highlighted the potential of AI to significantly interpret and generate information just like a human would.

Takeaways

Build With AI event was an enlightening experience that shed light on the immense possibilities offered by the integration of Gemini AI into applications. Attendees witnessed first hand how AI can generate information from prompts unlocking new avenues for creativity and knowledge acquisition.

Overall, the event showcased the transformative potential of AI in diverse domains, ranging from art to knowledge distribution. The integration of Gemini AI into applications opens up exciting possibilities for developers, enabling them to create intelligent tools that mimic human capabilities and deliver enhanced user experiences. Besides the technical skills, the event paved a great opportunity for attendees to network with each other.

As we move forward into an AI-driven future, events like the GDG Build With AI will serve as crucial platforms for promoting collaboration, innovation, and exploration. By embracing AI and harnessing its potential, we can unlock a world of limitless possibilities.
