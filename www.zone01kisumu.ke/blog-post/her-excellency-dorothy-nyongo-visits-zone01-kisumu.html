Her Excellency Dorothy Nyong'o visits Zone01 Kisumu

Written By:
Deril
Published On:
January 30, 2024
Share

We were glad to welcome the County Government of Kisumu First Lady, Her Excellency Mrs. Dorothy Nyong’o to Zone01 Kisumu today. Mama County was welcomed to the Zone by our Managing Director Dorcas Owino and Site Manager Caleb Nyoiro. She also met the entire Zone01 Kisumu team. 

Her Excellency took time to encourage interested applicants to complete their application to Zone01 Kisumu. She reiterated the support of the County Government towards our efforts to upskill, reskill and employ young people in Kisumu County at no cost. The County Government of Kisumu is a strategic partner of Zone01 Kisumu.

Earlier in the week, Mr. Wycliffe Ochiaga, the Managing Director Lake Basin Development Authority formally visited Zone01Kisumu. Our collaboration with the Authority is in line with Kenya's policy of promoting digital literacy and ICT skills among the population through training and employment programs. Zone01Kisumu is located at Lake Basin Mall, Mamboleo Junction.
