Technical Team Lead

About the role:

About Zone01 Kisumu

Zone01 Kisumu is a collective intelligence center who is revolutionizing the access to high-tech jobs in order to address the need for tech talent.

‍

Our selection process, based on motivation and merit, open to all backgrounds, requiring no diploma or previous knowledge in programming and is followed by a two-year intensive program based on an innovative curriculum in software engineering and programming, without tuition fees and a guaranteed employment contract after training.

‍

Our pedagogical system, created by our co-founder Nicolas Sadirac and our Tech team, is based on peer-learning and collective intelligence. Collective Intelligence Centers that run on the 01edu platform have no classes and no teachers, and students personalize their learning schedule through a gamified learning experience. After ending the program, we ensure students' employment into top tech jobs, by connecting the market with our international Talent network, ensuring Talent and Companies aspirations and expectations are met.

‍

What you will be doing

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Head of Tech Team (Bocal) to join our organization and play a pivotal role in transforming the education landscape of our country. You will work closely with our local and international teams, to ensure the successful delivery of our curriculum in alignment with our pedagogical approach. Additionally, you will oversee the maintenance and management of our school's equipment and infrastructure, as well as lead the mentors team.

‍

What is The Bocal and why is it so central?

The “Bocal” (meaning “Jar” in French) is the central space dedicated to the campus's technical, educational, and administrative team, starting from the launch of the candidate recruitment campaign.

‍

This is the command center where the team manages the technical and then pedagogical operations, including the relationships with the candidates and the learners.
